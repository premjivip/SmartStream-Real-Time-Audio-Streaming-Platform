About SmartStream

SmartStream is a full-stack music streaming platform where users can browse and listen to songs, create playlists, and explore new music. Admins can upload tracks and manage content, while users enjoy a personalized experience.

Users register with a username, email, password, and gender. The platform uses JWT authentication for secure login and role-based access control. Songs and media files are streamed efficiently through integrated audio players.

The front-end uses HTML, CSS, and Thymeleaf for dynamic server-side rendering. The design is responsive, visually appealing, and easy to navigate. Buttons, inputs, and sections maintain a consistent color scheme for readability.

SmartStream aims to combine a great music experience with clean, modern design.
